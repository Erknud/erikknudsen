Erik Knudsen is a Professor of Communication Science at the Department of Information Science and Media Studies at the University of Bergen (UiB). Much of his research explores patterns and effects of news use and political communication, trust in journalism, exposure to like-minded information, and polarization and fragmentation.

He leads a work package at the [MediaFutures Research Centre](https://mediafutures.no) for Responsible Media Technology and Innovation, and is PI of the project **NEWSREC – The Double-edged Sword of News Recommenders' Impact on Democracy**, awarded through the highly competitive *Research Projects for Young Talents* program of the Research Council of Norway.

He is founder of the **Norwegian Journalism Panel** and the interdisciplinary **Research Group for Political Behaviour, Democracy and Communication (PADKOM)** at DIGSSCORE. He also serves as editor-in-chief of *Norsk medietidsskrift* (the Norwegian Journal of Media Studies) and is a member of the **Young Academy of Norway**.
